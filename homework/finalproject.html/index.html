My name is Monika Rathod and I am starting my sophomore year at the University of Miami! I wrote this script during the first semester of college. It was one of my final projects that I had to complete. It took me over a month to figure out exactly what kind of characters I wanted, what the conflict was going to be, what my emotional turning points were going to be, and what I wanted my conclusion to be. It turned out to be one of the best scripts that I have written. If you would like to read it, it's linked down below! I hope you enjoy and don't hesistate to contact me with your thoughts, ideas, or questions.
