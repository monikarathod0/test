Olivia Robbins

Olivia Robbins is a 24 year old workaholic who is distressed everyday. The diner that she works for gives her constant anxiety and discomfort. Almost everyday, she gets sexually assaulted, walked over, and disrespected by two of her male co-workers. Olivia starts off a young girl who is degraded as a woman. Once Olivia gets kidnapped and imprisoned by her stalker, Bryan, she ends up fighting for her life. She ends up becoming someone stronger that she ever thought she could be.

Bryan Davis

Bryan Davis is a mid-30 year old man who has been coming to the diner that Olivia works at almost everyday. He ends up stalking her for almost 3 years, even though he was viewed as every other normal guy you see on the street. He lures Olivia into his car one night when she was closing up from her job and ends up kidnapping her. He does horrendous things to people that Olivia personally knows, exemplifying his major psychological issues. Will he keep Olivia trapped or will he get what he deserves?
